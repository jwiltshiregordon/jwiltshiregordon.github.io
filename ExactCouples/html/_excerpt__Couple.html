ExactCouples :: excerptCouple

excerptCouple -- displays one of the long exact sequences in an exact couple

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    excerptCouple(deg, k, C)
  • Inputs:
    • C, a module, an exact couple given as a module for a couple ring Q
    • deg, a list, an exterior multidegree of C, the starting degree; should be an even degree in the sense of Q.isEvenDegree(deg). This degree appears at the bottom of the middle column in the displayed long exact sequence
    • k, an integer, the number of winds of the long exact sequence to display
  • Outputs:
    • a net, the long exact sequence starting in degree deg and continuing for 3*k terms

The middle column of the displayed long exact sequence is in even degrees; in other words, its entries appear on the E_1 page of the couple. Specifically, $E_1^{pq} = Ext^p(W,A_q/A_{q-1})$ can be found in degree $(2p,2q)$. The bottom element of the middle column is degree $(0,4)$, which is then $Ext^0(W,A_2/A_1)$. The top element of the middle column is $Ext^1(W,A_2/A_1)$.
